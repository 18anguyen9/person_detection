Evaluation Metrics

Recall Curve

Recall focuses on detecting positives which will lead to False positives if recall is too high. In our case, recall is significant as our model needs to detect each person in the photo. But focusing only on recall may lead the model to classifying many non-human objects as people.

Precision Curve

Precision focuses on the reliability of the model. Precision focuses on the accuracy of all the objects classified as people. A high Precision score may be accurate in the objects classified, but focusing only on precision may miss non classified people in the photo

F1 Score

The F1 score is a combination of precision and recall. It gives us a quick summary of both the precision and recall scores. Using the F1, Precision, and Recall Scores will help us find the optimal balance between Precision and Recall
